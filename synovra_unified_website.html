Why Revival First?

Traditional battery recycling happens too early. Synovra's innovative approach extends battery life before recycling becomes necessary, creating true sustainability.

⚠️

The Problem

Traditional battery recycling creates environmental waste and loses valuable materials. Most batteries are discarded when they still have significant recoverable capacity.

  • Premature battery disposal increases electronic waste
  • Valuable lithium and rare earth materials are lost
  • High environmental cost of mining new materials
  • Energy-intensive recycling processes
  • Short battery lifecycles drive replacement demand
✨

The Solution

Synovra's battery revival technology extends battery life by up to 85% through advanced restoration processes. Our smart diagnostics identify recoverable capacity and restore batteries to like-new performance.

  • Extend battery life by 85% on average
  • Reduce electronic waste by 50%
  • AI-powered diagnostics and restoration
  • 3x lifespan multiplier for processed batteries
  • Sustainable approach to battery management
